Monetary Explainers

Bond yields are at multi-decade highs. Foreign central banks are net sellers of US Treasuries for the first time in a generation. The 1974 deal that put dollars in every oil barrel is unwinding in real time. This series builds the whole arc from first principles — bond math, reserve-currency mechanics, Triffin’s dilemma, the petrodollar loop, term premium, fiscal dominance — with widgets that prove each claim, not just describe it.
